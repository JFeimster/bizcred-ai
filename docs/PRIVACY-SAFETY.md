# Privacy & Safety Guidelines

BizCredit Builder OS is designed with user privacy and realistic expectations as top priorities.

## 1. Local-First Architecture
- **Data Stays in the Browser:** All profile information, tradelines, and roadmap progress are saved exclusively to the user's local browser storage (`localStorage`).
- **No Backend Storage:** In this phase of the application, there are no databases, backend servers, or external APIs collecting or persisting user data.
- **User Control:** Users have complete control over their data. They can export their data as a JSON file for safekeeping, import a previous state, or permanently delete all local data using the built-in reset tools.

## 2. Educational Scope
- **No Financial/Legal Advice:** This tool is strictly for educational and organizational planning. It does not provide financial, legal, tax, or credit repair advice.
- **Not a Credit Repair Tool:** The application does not "fix" bad credit, dispute items on credit reports, or interact with credit bureaus on the user's behalf.

## 3. No Guarantees
- **No Approval Guarantees:** Following the roadmap or achieving a high "Credit Architect Score" does not guarantee approval for credit lines, loans, or funding.
- **No Funding Promises:** The tool makes no promises regarding the amount of funding a user might secure.
- **No Score Increase Promises:** The tool does not guarantee that following its steps will result in a specific increase in Paydex, Intelliscore, or other business credit scores.

## 4. Vendor Reporting Verification
- **Verify Directly:** The vendor directory and planner are provided as a starting point. Vendor underwriting requirements and bureau reporting behaviors change frequently.
- **User Responsibility:** Users must verify directly with vendors about their current reporting practices before relying on them to build their credit profile. The application explicitly tracks "Verification Status" to encourage this behavior.
