Objection Crusher FAQ

Questions before you launch.

Clear expectations. No miracle claims.

What does BizCredit Builder help with?

It helps organize your business-credit setup, identify common readiness gaps, sequence next steps, and generate checklists or prompts for follow-up.

Is this credit repair?

No. It is an educational business-credit planning assistant, not a credit repair service.

Can it guarantee business credit approval?

No. It does not guarantee approvals, funding, tradelines, score changes, or lender outcomes.

What info should I bring?

Your entity type, formation date, EIN status, banking status, business address, phone, website, vendor history, goals, and known blockers.

Is this beginner-friendly?

Yes. It is built for founders and small business owners who want the moving pieces explained in a practical sequence.

What do I get back?

You can get readiness audits, action plans, vendor prep lists, question sets, follow-up prompts, and next-7-move checklists.

Can I use it before applying for vendors or funding?

Yes. That is one of the best use cases: spot what may need review before you chase applications or conversations.

What should I do after using it?

Review the checklist, verify details with qualified professionals where needed, complete missing setup items, and iterate with follow-up prompts.
